# Issue-Spotting Analysis for Med-Mal Intake

**Physician-led medical record review that tells you which cases have merit -- before you invest in litigation.**

MedMal Review Pro provides structured, physician-authored issue-spotting memos for plaintiff medical malpractice firms. Each case is reviewed by a board-certified emergency medicine physician who identifies deviations from standard of care, documentation vulnerabilities, and defensibility weaknesses -- the clinical detail your team needs to make confident intake decisions.

---

## What the Report Includes

- **Standard of care analysis** -- specific clinical guidelines that were or were not followed
- **Timeline of critical events** -- chronological breakdown of key decision points
- **Documentation gaps** -- missing notes, unsigned orders, contradictory records
- **Defensibility assessment** -- how a defense expert would likely characterize the care
- **Causation notes** -- connection between identified deviations and patient outcome
- **Supporting literature** -- relevant clinical guidelines and practice standards cited
- **Plain-language summary** -- non-clinical overview for case evaluation meetings

---

## How It Works

**1. Submit** -- Send de-identified medical records through our secure intake portal or encrypted email. No minimum page count. We handle disorganized records.

**2. Review** -- A board-certified physician reviews the complete record, identifies potential issues, and produces a structured memo. Turnaround: 48-72 hours for standard cases.

**3. Decide** -- Use the memo to make an informed go/no-go decision on the case. If you proceed, the memo serves as a foundation for your medical expert strategy.
