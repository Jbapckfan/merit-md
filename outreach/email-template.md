# Attorney Outreach Email Template

## Subject Line Variants (A/B/C Test)

**A:** Reducing your med-mal case screening time by 70%
**B:** ER physician-built chart review for plaintiff firms
**C:** Free: sample medical record analysis on your next intake

---

## Email Body

Hi {{first_name}},

I know your firm screens dozens of potential med-mal cases each month. Most don't survive past intake -- but figuring out which ones have merit still takes expensive physician review time.

I built a structured issue-spotting process specifically for plaintiff intake. I'm a board-certified emergency medicine physician, and I review the medical records myself -- looking for deviations from standard of care, documentation gaps, and defensibility weaknesses that matter to your case theory.

Here's my offer: send me one de-identified case file. I'll send back a structured issue-spotting memo within 48 hours. Free. No strings.

If the memo is useful, we can talk about what ongoing support looks like. If it's not, you've lost nothing.

Just reply to this email and I'll send over the intake instructions.

Best,
{{sender_name}}, MD
Board-Certified Emergency Medicine
MedMal Review Pro

---

## Usage Notes

- Send as plain text only (no HTML, no links, no attachments)
- Personalize {{first_name}} with the managing partner or intake director name
- Send from a personal-looking address (e.g., firstname@meritmd.com), not a marketing address
- Follow up once at day 5 if no reply, then move on
- Track opens/replies per subject line variant to determine winner
- Volume: 10-15 per day max to avoid spam filters
- Best send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 7:30-9:00 AM local time

## Word Count

Body is ~140 words (under 150 target).
